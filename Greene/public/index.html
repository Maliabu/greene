    On-going Management

    Management Fee

    The management fee is charged on all monies collected on your behalf. Management fees cover the day to day running of the property, tenant and landlord, arranging maintenance, site visits and collecting and processing rental payments

    Routine Inspections

    Inspections are carried out every three months and a report of inspection is forwarded to the landlord. Ugent maintenance issues will be reported to you immediately and seek your advice on the necessary action to take

    Maintenance and Repairs

    We readily have seasoned technics to carry out all necessary repairs and maintenance as the need shall arise. Costs for such repairs or maintenance shall be computed into the management fee

    Water and Electricity Arrears

    Such fees shall fall to the tenant. The company shall however be responsible for sewers ( with the guidance of the landlord) depending on the estate in question.

    Accounts

    At the client's request, we can pay for all your accounts including investment property, council rates, water rates, maintenance etc. Copies of receipts are sent to the landlord with the end of the month statement

    End of the month statements

    At the end of the month, a shall be issued to the lamdlord with details on the following:

  • Rental fees collected
  • All outgoings (rates and maintenance)
  • Management fees
  • Monies forwarded to the bank
    • × A detailed end of the month, a statement capturing all
    Tenant education

    This is a crucial part of management of the property. The education is aimed at equiping the tenant with the skills and knowledge on how to maintain amenities, ensure that less is spent on maintenance and repairs. We therefore educate tenants on various relevant issues including: relighting a hot water system, operating air conditioning systems, operating the stoves and how to cut costs including electricity and water rates.

    Security and Hygiene

    We provide the option of 24 hour security guards and dedicated Professional cleaners to ensure the smooth and comfortable stay of occupants and the maintenance of the property in highly hygienic standards
