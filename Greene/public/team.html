Mission

Greene Estates and property solutions is a full service real-estate development and management company in Uganda offering resillient and long-term solutions to our clients. Our services aim at understanding, anticipating and ably meeting client's needs in an accurate, timely and cost effective manner. We ensure that prices negotiated are most profitable and fair to all parties. We believe in tean work. Our agents are a group of highly motivated young people, who understand the value of hard work, excellence and trust, working collectively and combining talents to make an impact in their communities.

Our highest aspiration is to make your dream come true

Who we are?

Greene Estates property solutions was started in 2019 by a group of organised ang young people with a background in law, real estates management and business.

Our conviction is that the housing deficit in Uganda can be solved with efficient solutions crafted around technology, professionalism and trustworthy systems.
